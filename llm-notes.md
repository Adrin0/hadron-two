# Notes on Different LLMs

## LLMs

### chatgpt - OpenAI
* Most token cost for highest quality output
* Strong general-purpose reasoning and instruction-following
* Large plugin/tool ecosystem
* Good at structured outputs (JSON, tables, formatted reports)

### claude(code) - Anthropic
* 50% cheaper than chatgpt but equally or more quality output
* Excels at long-context tasks (large codebases, long documents)
* More cautious and less likely to hallucinate confidently
* Best-in-class for agentic/coding workflows via Claude Code

### grok - Elon
* cool for integrating with x.com but not really good at generative stuff. 
* keeps you up to date with trendning topics
* Real-time X/Twitter data access is its main differentiator
* Weaker at complex reasoning compared to GPT-4 or Claude

### copilot - Msft
* only llm compatible with imss?
* pretty good at simple automation and light generative content.
* Get's better at repeated tasks
* Deeply integrated into Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, Teams)
* Powered by OpenAI models under the hood

### gemini - Google
* best for quick searches
* Native multimodal (text, image, audio, video)
* Tight integration with Google Workspace and Search
* Gemini Advanced rivals GPT-4 for complex tasks

### ollama - Meta
* free and opensource?
* Runs locally — no data leaves your machine
* Great for privacy-sensitive or air-gapped environments
* Performance depends heavily on your local hardware
